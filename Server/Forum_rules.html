This is the forum rules page.

  • General
    1. Use common sense. It's impossible to list all the things you're not allowed to do. The rules below are not acting as a complete list of bannable offenses - instead they aim to clear up some confusion about those rules that may not be obvious.
    2. Chronet is aimed at a general audience. Please refrain from posting vulgar responses, drug-related topics, nudity, porn, and explicit content. Such contact hidden in spoiler tags is not allowed as well.
    3. Keep mini-modding to a minimum. Help is always appreciated, but please do not take problems into your own hands. Please use the report button. Refrain from responses such as, but not limited to: "Reported", "Wrong Section", etc.
    4. This is an English speaking forum. All messages, posts, titles, signatures, and content must be in English.
  • Posting
    1. Topic titles must be valid and appropriate to the thread content. Please refrain from posting "HELP!!11" or use of all caps.
    2. Treat others positively and with respect. Trolling and flaming is obviously not allowed. Disrespect may result in a warning or a ban depending on the offense.
    3. Topics must be submitted to the relevant forum section. Please read the forum descriptions before posting.
    4. Bumping is allowed provided that the reply has relevant information that is useful to the continuance of the topic. Short intervals of bumps are not allowed to bump the topic up onto the first page by the same person.
    5. It is generally forbidden to double post in a thread. It means that you are not allowed to reply twice in a row in any topic. Use the edit button when altering your reply if you're the last to post in the topic.
    6. Personal information posted in public, as well as false impersonations, illegal content, or selling of a person confidential or personal information will result in a permanent ban.
    7. One-Liner responses such as "+1", "lol", the use of only an emoticon. etc are not allowed. Use the rating system instead.
    8. The discussion of warez is fine, however posting links, asking for links, hinting at where to download it, etc is not allowed.
    9. Keep image macros and memes to a minimum. Excessive use will result in a warning or ban.
    10. '4chan' or '9gag' behaviour is strictly forbidden. Such behaviour will result in a ban.
  • Profiles and signatures
    1. Avatars may not contain nudity, porn, gore, any explicit content, or any "NSFW" content.
    2. Staff icons, symbols, or images are prohibited.
    3. All avatars must be distinct from ones currently in use by a staff members to refrain from possible confusion or impersonation.
    4. Signatures are restricted to 800 pixels wide by 150 pixels height (Maximum).
    5. Signatures may not contain unapproved advertising, asking/begging for "likes", or links to sites containing illegal activity.
    6. Affliates or referral links are prohibited. (Unless permission is given)
    7. Prohibited content for both avatars are signatures are, but is not limited to: graphical or explicit content, advertisements/referrals, sexual content, gore, any "NSFW" content.
  • Warning system
    • The forums use a points-based warning system.
    • Moderators may issue users infraction points for any forum conduct that breaches these rules, or is otherwise thought to be inappropriate.
    • These points may be issued in varying quantities, and may expire after a certain length of time, depending on the moderator's discretion.
    • Accumulating infraction points can lead to the following punishments:
      • 10 points = 1 day ban.
      • 20 points = 1 week ban.
      • 30 points = 1 month ban.
      • 40 points = Permanent ban.
    • A permanent forum ban is treated as an expuslion from the community, so the user is also permanently banned from our servers.
