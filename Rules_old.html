1. Chat and communication
    1. Respect your fellow players within the server. Players will not do any of the following:
      1. Flame each other:
        1. Flaming is a hostile and insulting interaction between one or more people, often involving the use of profanity.
        2. Flaming is an argument that becomes nasty or derisive, where insulting a party to the discussion takes precedence over the objective merits of one side or another.
        3. Do not continue to "flame" after an admin tells you to stop.
      2. Disrespect any other player on the server
      3. Unnecessary and excessive arguing.
      4. Be racist or homophobic or discriminate against any race, sex, etc.
      5. No spamming in any form.
      6. Advertising in OOC. This includes advertising other servers.
      7. Use of the '/advert' chat command for anything other than roleplaying.
      8. Disrespect an admins authority in any way.
    2. Players must not abuse voice chat.
      1. Spamming the microphone in any way is not allowed.
      2. Make any inappropriate sounds after receiving a complaint is not allowed.
      3. You may only play music over your microphone if you are roleplaying with the music (Eg. Playing music on stage) or if you are a Radio Broadcaster
    3. Metagaming.
      1. Metagaming is when you use out of character knowledge, to roleplay.
      2. Do not use OOC chat to metagame.
    4. Party chat
      1. The party system is purely for in character communication.
      2. All other chat rules still apply in party chat.
    5. Do not reveal admins that are in disguise.
    6. Demote function
      1. You may only demote players for not doing their jobs correctly, not breaking rules. Please contact an admin if a player is breaking rules.

  2. Props
    1. Players will not abuse the use of props. The following is considered as prop abuse / prop minging:
      1. Prop blocking. The following is considered to be prop blocking:
        1. Obstructing or blocking of any public areas.
        2. Making a base in accessible by placing props in front of entrances without a working keypad.
        3. Preventing the ability of accessing a entity, item, etc. without a working keypad.
      2. Prop killing. Prop killing is defined as:
        1. Forcefully colliding another player with a prop, causing damage or death.
      3. Prop pushing. Prop pushing is defined as:
        1. Forcefully colliding a prop with another player in order to obstruct their gameplay.
        2. Forcefully colliding yourself or another player with a prop which will allow them to access a place in the map which not normally accessible by normal gameplay.
      4. Prop surfing. Prop surfing is defined as:
        1. Using props to forcefully launch yourself into the air.
        2. 'Surfing' on a prop in mid air.
        3. Placing props without proper support as it would have in real life.
      5. Prop climbing. Prop climbing is defined as:
        1. Spawning props, with the intention of using them reach otherwise inaccessible places.
        2. In certain RP situations, props such as ladders can be used to RP. (Eg. Using a prop ladder to get onto a roof. You would however, not use a ladder to climb up a barb wired fence).
      6. Prop shielding. Prop shielding is defined as:
        1. Using a prop to shield yourself from a gun fight in a unrealistic manner.
      7. Prop trapping. Prop trapping is defined as:
        1. Placing props around a player so that the player is unable to exit.
        2. Prop trapping is allowed if done in a RP way.

  3. Base construction
    1. Fading Doors
      1. Any area of your base must be no more than 2 fading door/keypad combinations away. This rule is taken account from the first entrance to your base (Eg. Front door) to the final destination of anyone entering. Example
        1. Your first fading door may be a 'security gate'. This is usually placed in front of or behind the entrance (door) of your base. It must be realistically placed.
        2. Your second fading door must used on safe/container. A safe/container is defined as a small area in which your contraband or items are placed in. Minimal base defending will be done from inside a container. It is used only to house your items.
      2. You must have either a button or keypad for each fading door.
        1. Fading doors must stay open (Toggled) once the button/keypad is pressed. (Ie. It may not have a timer which automatically makes it go off after x seconds)
        2. Keypads or buttons need to be visible, accessible and their function has to be clear. They must be clearly visible next to your fading door. They may not be invisible
      3. You may not make fading door bridges.
    2. All bases must be accessible by at least one entrance.
      1. The same rule applies for shops.
      2. If there are multiple doors going into your shop/house you may block all but one of them.
      3. Trap doors are not considered as a main entrance. (Ie. You may not only have a trap door as the entrance to your base. There must be another entrance available)
    3. Shooting windows:
      1. The minimum size for a shooting window is 2 tiles high and 8 tiles long. (A 1x1 PHX block is 4x4 tiles) Example 1 Example 2
      2. Shooting windows must be placed at least 4 tiles above the ground. (No windows or gaps for shooting people in the feet when they can't shoot back)
      3. The window must be the maximum height possible but without letting a player crouch through it. Use this as reference when making shooting windows.
      4. All shooting windows must have a button on the shooters side of the window.
      5. Shooting windows may not be small slits.
      6. Shooting windows must be textured/colored as see through.
    4. Props used to build your base must obey the following rules:
      1. Prop mazing is not allowed. Prop mazing is defined as:
        1. Creating mazes with props to slow down players getting into your property.
        2. Making players duck, jump or climb over props to slow them from getting into your property.
        3. Forcing a player to go through a certain area or path to get their final destination (Ie. Your contraband, etc)
      2. Pure white/black materials may not be used on structures/props/bases, unless for RP purposes.
      3. No creating invisible props.
      4. Certain props are not allowed to be materialized. Do not try to avoid the system in place by using dupes, etc. (This applies to fences, amongst other props)
      5. You may in no way create props or bases that abuse the bullet penetration system.
        1. If you create a prop that bullets will penetrate, they must be able to penetrate all sides of the prop.
      6. You may not use the "No physics property" on any of your props.
      7. You may not use "no collide" on any of your props. (Unless used for pure roleplay purposes)
    5. Base location:
      1. Do not build bases in buildings clearly made for shops.
      2. Your base may not block off any public area (Such as roads, roof tops, tunnels, PD lobby, etc).
      3. You may not build bases in the Night Club.
    6. Kill zones/boxes/ropes/lines are not allowed.
    7. You may not use use lights, lamps, material or any other tool to purposely lag or disorient players.
    8. Do not create any kind of sky lift or a house in the middle of the sky.
    9. Do not buy property that you are not using.

  4. General Roleplay
    1. Bad roleplay/Fail Roleplay is not allowed. The following are defined as bad roleplay:
      1. Continuously breaking laws to get attention from police or Government Officials.
      2. Running or driving away from cops for doing petite crimes (Such as breaking a window, running a red light for no reason, etc).
      3. Doing something completely unrealistic.
      4. Scamming players is not allowed.
    2. Do not punch whore. Punch whoring will not be tolerated.
    3. Follow Fear RP. You can't attack a person who has a gun pointed at you, while you are unarmed. You must roleplay realism and be afraid of the gun.
    4. You may not steal a person's gun or item over the counter in a shop. After they have left the shop, you may do as you want.
    5. You must have a proper RP reason to throw a molotov.
    6. Do not do unrealistic things when dead/unconscious. This includes talking over voice chat, typing in local chat, PMing people, party chat, 911, etc. You may only use OOC chat when dead/unconscious.

  5. New Life Rule (NLR)
    1. When you die and are not revived, an NLR zone will be created around your place of death. You will also be put into recovery for 120 seconds when you spawn. Your NLR zone will last 3 minutes. You may not enter it. You will be slayed if you enter it without leaving.
    2. Do not break New Life Rule (NLR)
      1. The NLR comes into effect when a player dies. You do not remember anything about how or where you died.
    3. If you die by an accident then you are excluded from NLR and may return to where you died.
    4. If you are killed by another player intentionally then you may NOT return to where you were killed until 5 minutes is up.
    5. Civilians
      1. If you die on your property, you must wait a minimum of 5 minutes before returning. If however you return after 5 minutes and see the same situation still occurring (with the same players), you must leave and return when it is finished.
      2. You must wait 5 minutes before returning to your place of death.
    6. Government Officials
      1. You must wait 5 minutes before returning to your place of death.
      2. You may return to your place of death after 5 minutes is up and a backup call has been received.

  6. Cheating
    1. Cheating is defined as the following:
      1. Using wallhacks, aimbots, ESPs, ghosting, excessive binds, etc.
      2. Exploiting glitches or bugs to your advantage. Report all glitches/exploits to an admin or on the forums. Failure to do so may result in a permanent ban.
      3. Exploiting the map by going outside it, underneath it, or inside the sky box.
      4. Anti-AFKing.
      5. Using macros, auto-clockers, configs, etc.
    2. Alternate accounts are not allowed. You may only have one Steam account to play on the server with. Any others will be permanently banned and your primary account may also receive the same punishment.
    3. Combat logging is not allowed.
      1. Combat logging is disconnecting from the server in order to avoid recovery, jail time, or loss of items.

  7. Gangs, groups, parties, organisations
    1. Players should use the party system in order to make gangs/groups/factions/etc.
    2. Do not metagame with party/org/group chat.
    3. Jobs within gangs
      1. Government Officials may not join gangs.
        1. Secret Agents may however join a gang if they are on an "undercover mission"

  8. Raiding
    1. The raider(s) must obey the following rules when raiding another player:
      1. You may use very minimal prop barricades when raiding (Eg. Concrete blocks)
        1. You may not create these barricades while in a gun fight. (Must be made before hand)
      2. Do not raid players in the process of building their base. This rule is invalid if the player's base has farming items inside (Contraband, stoves, weed, etc).
      3. You can not kill unarmed people in raids, unless they are interfering or calling the cops. Tell them to face the wall and cooperate or to move out of the way. If they disobey your commands or if they block doors or interfere, you are free to kill them. This does not apply to the owners of a base you raid.
    2. The player(s) being raided must obey the following the rules while the raid is in process:
      1. The opening and closing of your own recently cracked fading doors during raid is not allowed.
        1. This includes the usage of the player's keyboard number pad, keypads and buttons.
      2. You may not rebuild or repair your base while a raid is taking place.
      3. Do not lock spam. This is defined as repeatedly hitting your lock key while someone is lockpicking your door
      4. Do not door spam. This is defined as repeatedly press E on a door that is unlocked, therefore disabling the raiders to enter.
      5. Shooting windows may only be 'activated' or 'faded' once every 5 seconds. (Ie. The cooldown period between pressing the button attached to your shooting windows' fading door is 5 seconds)
    3. Police Raids
      1. You may not raid somebody purely based on sounds/effect created by items.
      2. If an item explodes inside a home, you may forcefully check the person's home for the source of the explosion.

  9. General Street Crime, Hostages, Mugging
    1. General Street Crime
      1. You may only commit street crime in realistic situations.
        1. This includes realistic areas and enviroments. (Eg. A realistic time to commit street crime would be at night. There would be little or mininal witnesses.)
        2. As a criminal, one of your primary objectives is to not get caught. Do not commit crime in the middle of the day in plain sight of police officers and/or multiple witnesses.
      2. Do not steal, rob, extort, etc without a valid RP reason.
      3. Do not Fail RP. All criminals have a background story of some kind. If you are a 'low-end catburglar' criminal, you will not be in a huge base with the latest weapons and tech.
    2. Hostages
      1. Hostages must be tied with a rope.
        1. Be realistic when tied. Although the gamemode blocks you from doing certain things, refrain from doing unrealistic things when tied.
      2. You may roleplay to take all items from a hostage (Unless they say otherwise, do not power game)
      3. The maximum ransom amount you can ask for is $2000. (Or $10 000 city funds)
      4. Do not run away while a gun is being pointed at you. This is breaking FearRP.
      5. Once your demands/ransom have been met, you must release the hostage.
      6. You must have a good roleplay reason to hold somebody hostage. Don't randomly take people hostage.
      7. After 10 minutes, the hostage(s) may choose to no longer participate in the hostage situation. You must let them go if they wish to leave after 10 minutes.
    3. Mugging
      1. As with street crime, you may only mug players in realistic areas. (Eg. A dark alley way where no other people are)
      2. You may only mug a player for $200 at once.
      3. You may not mug the same person within 30 minutes.

  10. Random Deathmatch
    1. Do not random deathmatch. That means killing a person, or multiple people, without a reason.
      1. What is RDM:
        1. Killing a player that is not a threat to you.
        2. Killing a player on sight without reason.
        3. Killing wanted players without prior warning.
        4. Killing a player as a hit if you are not a hitman.
        5. Killing the mayor without a proper RP reason.
          1. You may only kill the mayor for the following reasons: (Unless an admin says otherwise)
            1. If the Mayor has been in power for 10 or more minutes and has caused the economy to loose a large amount of economy points and is now less than 0 points. (Ie. Less than half the economy bar)
            2. If the Mayor keeps the taxes high for a long period of time and rejects peoples complains of them being too high.
            3. If you are roleplaying kidnapping the Mayor (For a good roleplay reason or a reason specified above) and the Mayor does not meet your demands or the Police do not obey your commands.
        6. Killing a Government Official that previously arrested you. (Unless RPed correctly)
      2. What is not RDM:
        1. Killing a player after being attacked.
        2. Killing a player for killing your friend, within 5 minutes of your friend's death.
        3. Killing a player for putting a hit on you.
        4. Killing police while wanted.
        5. Killing police while you are being chased. (Must not be a 'petite' crime)
        6. Killing police while a friend is being pursued. (Must not be a 'petite' crime)
        7. Killing a player inside your property.
        8. Killing a player by accident during a gunfight, as long as you apologize.
        9. Killing a player for stealing your items.
        10. Killing a player for breaking into your base.
    2. Friends:
      1. A friend is a player that is in the same gang/party as you.
      2. A friend is a player that is in your organisation.
      3. A friend is a player that you have a base with.
      4. A friend is not a player you have never met.
      5. A friend is not a player you have known for a short amount of time

  11. Jobs Group Rules
    1. Civilian (green on scoreboard)
      1. You may do as you please. You are free to particape in any activity you see fit (with obvious excpetions). You can roleplay as criminal or otherwise.
    2. Government Officials (blue on scoreboard)
      1. You may not be corrupt. Do your job properly or you will be blacklisted/banned.
      2. You may not arrest/warrant/kill any player who does not follow server rules. Please report those players to an admin. An exception can be made for prop pushers/killer when there is no admin on.
      3. You may arrest people who do not follow the laws of the city.
      4. It is important to follow the NLR as a cop. If you are killed don't go back and arrest them.
      5. Do not kill fellow Government Officials.
      6. You may not have money printers or any illegal items.
      7. Secret Agents may not act as normal police. (May not patrol streets for crime, etc). Agents should do mission based work.
      8. Government officials may not arrest or kill other government officials.
      9. If you receive word of a hostage situation, do not run straight in and shoot randomly. Handle hostage situation realistically.
      10. If you spot illegal items as an Emergency Response Unit, inform Police. Do not destroy them yourself.
      11. Do not randomly taze, handcuff, or search people.
      12. Combat arresting is not allowed. Use tasers and handcuffs.
      13. You may not base alone or with others. You are allowed a house.
      14. You may not use personal vehicles to drive around in, unless you are not on duty.

  12. Jobs Specific Rules
    1. Citizen
      1. You are allowed to roleplay any job or character you wish (within reason).
    2. Bus Driver
      1. You must do your job. This means that during workhours (day time), you must drive around citizens of Rockford.
      2. It is optional for you to work at night. You may either continue doing your job, or do something else.
    3. Road Service Worker
      1. You must do your job. This means that you must work all hours of the day to provide tow truck and repair services to citizens of Rockford. You are allowed an hour or two per RP day to do as you wish.
      2. You may not use your tow truck in malicious or illegal activities.
    4. News Camerman
      1. You must do your job. Provide the citizens of Rockford with news. You are allowed an hour or two personal time per day.
    5. Mayor
      1. In the event that there are no Secret Service agents, you may assign other Government Officials to be your guards.
      2. You may fire Government Officials if they do not do their job or if they disobey you.
      3. You are allowed to roleplay as a "bad" Mayor. It is however best to fisrt ask an admins permission to roleplay as one.
    6. Police Chief
      1. You may give orders to the police force.
      2. If there is a Mayor, you take orders from him.
    7. Police Officer
      1. You are allowed to patrol with your squad car.
      2. You must obey orders given by the Police Chief.
    8. SWAT
      1. You may not patrol with your SWAT van. You must park it somewhere and wait for a backup call from other Government Officials.
    9. Emergency Response Unit
      1. You are not allowed to heal sides during a gun fight or raid. (Ie. Only heal Civilians, or only heal Government). You may prioritise healing Government.
    10. Detective
      1. You are allowed to drive around in your personal vehicle if you see fit.
    11. Secret Service
      1. You must do everything the Mayor says. You may not patrol or do your own missions.

  13. Premium Members
    1. Although Premium Members have special privileges for supporting the server, the privileges must not be abused.
    2. You will have the same punishments if you disobey the rules as guests do.
    3. You may not abuse your votekick:
      1. You may only votekick players for serious rulebreaking (for example: Mass RDM'ers and propminges).
      2. You may not votekick players for minor rulebreaking (a single RDM, metagaming, NLR).
      3. You have to warn a player at least once before you votekick him, and give him a chance to improve himself.
    4. You may not abuse 3D Signs:
      1. Signs should be used for RP only.
      2. Absolutely no NSFW or inappropriate images or text to be used on the signs. You will be given one warning, after which you will receive an immediate ban.

  14. Admins
    1. You must still follow all normal rules even you are admin.
    2. Do not noclip into people's houses. Only noclip when a issue comes up that needs attention.
    3. You must give a valid reason for banning/kicking. Do not use reasons such as "No" or "General idiot". The reason must explain why and what they were banned for. Do not use meme or 9gag reasons for your bans or kicks.
    4. Do not bother admins when you have no proof.
    5. When you must request an admin, do it in PM not OOC. You can also use @ <message>
    6. Do not noclip into your house or around the map. Only noclip when an issue comes up.
    7. Do not kick or ban other admins.
If ever in doubt, the main rule to follow is, "Am I negatively effecting and/or being unfair toward anyone/anyones gameplay?". If the answer is yes, then you may never do it. If the answer is no, you are usually allowed to do it. - Simple
